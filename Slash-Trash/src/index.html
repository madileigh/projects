    ABOUT US

    Slash Trash is a green company that is dedicated to reducing trash produced by food establishments on a daily basis. It promotes the use of reusable items by providing incentives to customers. We keep track of the reusable items used, points gained by the customers, and also the waste points saved by the establishments.

    HOW TO USE SLASH-TRASH

    • In the "Adding New Data" Section, find the table you want to add a record to and click "Insert 'Table_Name'", fill out the fields then click "Add".
    • In the "Update Data Section", find the table you want to update, click "Update 'Table_Name'", then fill out the fields and click "Update".
    • To Display data, select the table from the dropdown, and hit "Display Data".
    • To delete a record, select the table from the dropdown, provide the Id for the record, and click the "Delete" button.
    • To find how many points a particular customer has gained from the given range of dates, just provide the Customer Id, the Beginning Date, the End Date, then click "View".
    • Last but not least, to add bonus point(s) to customers who visited a particular Establishment on a particular date, provide the Establishment Id of the Establishment, the Date, and the bonus points to be added. Finally, click "Add" to update the original table.
